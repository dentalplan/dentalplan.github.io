Cliff Hammett is:

    a new media meddler
    a 'nose' philospher
    an occasional poet
    a sometime illustrator
    an errant diagrammer
    a creative tinkerer

I develop critical and playful investigations into a number of phenomena. These have included jokes, card games, genetic algorithms, co-word analyses, errant temporalities and many others. I tackle these individually, or with members of the Open Systems Association, of whom I'm an active member.

Alongside this, I've been working with Alexandra Joensson and the x:talk collective on the x_msg project, an SMS-based social software system for use by sex work activists. In 2011, we were given an Award of Distinction in the Digital Communities category at Prix Ars Electronica for x_msg.

I've longstanding interests in indeterminacy in complex systems, the [something] of animal life, different forms of time and several strains of ethics/political philosophy. I'm a practicing poet and illustrator, often producing work in collaboration with Sidekick Books and Fuselit magazine.
