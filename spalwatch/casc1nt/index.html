Analysis of segment of video from performance. Extracts are determined by the relative movements of the right and left hands to each other, with images taken at the points where the hands are closest and furthest from each other. Each image pair is illuminated according the level of change in the image. The composite images are cascaded, with the recorded video times displayed along side it.

The corresponding audio segment has likewise been broken up according to the movements of my hands. Parts have been sped up or slowed down, so that each recorded movement has the same amount of time given to it, and has been recombined into a single piece of the same length as the original.
